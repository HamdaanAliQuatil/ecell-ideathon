Ideathon '22 by Envision, IIIT Kalyani is an effort to nudge candidates to come up with innovative solution to some of the most imperative challenges of our time. Participants work in teams and use design thinking and innovative learning practices to ideate and collaborate on possible solutions. Students across various majors are encouraged to form a team so as to some up with a distinctive and ingenious solutions. The wider cross-section of students involved, the more perspectives there are to come up with insights that are relevant and novel. There are various tracks in which team may participate. Each track has it's own problems associated with them for which team are encouraged to ponder upon. The tracks are explained in elaborate detail below.
