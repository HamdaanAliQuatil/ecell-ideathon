Envision, E-Cell, IIIT Kalyani, is an upcoming club that aspires to thrive on the aspirations of the budding minds and induce in them an entrepreneurial mindset and help them discover their dormant streak. Envision aspires to cater to the eveready and passionate and help them explore the accrescent domain of business and entrepreneurship. It aims to inspire and help students at campus to venture into and explore the entrepreneurial domain in all it's glory and come up with innovative solutions to prevalent issues as an efficient model fro business or societal benefit. The paramount goal is to create a cohort of peers that push themselves to understand the various intricacies of business thinking and inculcate in them the urge to innovate, take risks, shoulder social responsibility and indulge in creative execution of ideas.
Ideathon '22 by Envision, IIIT Kalyani is an effort to nudge candidates to come up with innovative solution to some of the most imperative challenges of our time. Participants work in teams and use design thinking and innovative learning practices to ideate and collaborate on possible solutions. Students across various majors are encouraged to form a team so as to some up with a distinctive and ingenious solutions. The wider cross-section of students involved, the more perspectives there are to come up with insights that are relevant and novel. There are various tracks in which team may participate.
